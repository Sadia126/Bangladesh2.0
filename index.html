Pillars of"Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of turning creative ideas into practical solutions that add value. It drives progress by challenging norms and fostering new possibilities. .

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental physical and organizational structures needed for a society's operation, such as roads, bridges, utilities, and communication systems.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving individuals or communities the tools, knowledge, and confidence to take control of their lives and make meaningful decisions.
